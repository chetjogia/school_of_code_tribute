Jimi Hendrix

27 Nov 1942 - 18 Sep 1970

Jimi Hendrix is regarded as one of the most innovative guitarist's to have ever lived!

Here are some key facts about Jimi Hendrix's life

  • Hendrix was left handed, and played a right hand guitar upside down, with the strings restrung back to front.
  • Although an American national, Hendrix first made his break in the UK.
  • Hendrix was first a talented session guitarist, for artists such as Little Richard.
  • Hendrix served in the Vietnam War in the 101st Airborne Paratroopers.
  • He only release three albums.
  • Jimi Hendrix was actually born John Allen Hendrix. When his father returned home from war in 1945, he changed his name to James Marshall Hendrix
